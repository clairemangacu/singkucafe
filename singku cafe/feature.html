More than Coffee

Celebrating flavors, stories, and moments that make every visit to Singku special.

Beyond the Brew

At Singku Cafe, every cup of coffee is crafted to be more than just a drink — it’s an experience. We take pride in sourcing quality beans that bring out rich, smooth, and comforting flavors, whether you’re craving something bold like an Americano, sweet and creamy like our signature Iced Spanish Latte, or indulgent with a touch of chocolate or caramel. From hot brews that warm your mornings to iced creations that refresh your afternoons, our coffees are made to match your mood and moments. At Singku, we believe coffee is not only about taste but also about creating little pauses in life where you can slow down, breathe, and enjoy.

Events at Singku

At Singku, every event is crafted to spark creativity, build community, and celebrate culture. From soulful live art sessions and spoken word poetry nights to fun-filled trivia games and inspiring art print showcases, our café transforms into a hub of talent and togetherness. These events aren’t just activities — they’re shared moments that make Singku more than a café, but a space for stories, laughter, and connection.

Live Music Nights at Singku

Every week, Singku transforms into a stage for local talents. Our Live Music Nights celebrate soulful voices, acoustic vibes, and the rhythm of community. It’s not just music — it’s the heartbeat of the café, where friends gather, conversations flow, and every note creates memories.

Whether you’re a music lover, a dreamer, or simply someone looking for a cozy evening, our rooftop turns into your perfect stage-side seat. Come for the coffee, stay for the music, and leave with stories to tell.
